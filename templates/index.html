Hello there! My name is Mayank, and I'm a Computer Science major at the University of California, Irvine specializing in Intelligent Systems. I'm part of two research groups at my university: 1) researching the spread of wildfires in California with Convolutional Neural Networks and 2) researching synthetic network generators. This site is both a portfolio for the things I'm working on as well as a means to document some of my technical thoughts. Thanks for stopping by!

 

  • UwUsic

    • An award winner at HackUCI! This site uses machine learning + facial recognition with face-api.js to detect your mood and generate a Spotify playlist to match using your preferred artists and genre. I trained an SVM model that takes song data from Spotify's API and predicts the levels of happiness, sadness, and anger present in each song's tone.

  • COVID-19 Media Topic Assessments

    • Top 10 at MIT's Data Hackathon! This research project uses unsupervised topic modeling on social media platforms and media reports at-large to learn the priorities of public concerns relative to the number of COVID-19 cases and major developments.

  • PeterPortal

    • As a member of the ICS Student Council at UCI, we're working on a comprehensive 4-year course planning tool that will help students access professor reviews, course prerequisite trees, personal progress, and more all in one place. We are currently getting feedback from alpha testing right now here!
